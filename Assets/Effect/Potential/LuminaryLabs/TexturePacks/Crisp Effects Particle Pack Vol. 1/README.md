# Crisp Effect Particle Pack Vol. 1

## What This Pack Is

Crisp Effect Particle Pack Vol. 1 is a texture pack for building sharp, stylized particles, hit effects, bursts, impact accents, decals, and UI/VFX details. It includes 50 original Luminary Labs crisp effect textures in multiple sizes, 150 animated flipbook variants, starter materials for URP and mobile workflows, and demo content for quick preview and setup.

## What's Included

- Static crisp effect textures:
  - `CrispEffects_256/`
  - `CrispEffects_512/`
  - `CrispEffects_1024/`
  - `CrispEffects_2048/`
- Flipbook textures:
  - `CrispEffects_Flipbooks/` (`x4`, `x8`, and `x16` variants)
- Starter materials:
  - `CrispEffects_Materials/`
  - Mobile additive and alpha variants
  - URP Lit additive and alpha variants
  - URP Unlit additive and alpha variants
- Demo content:
  - `CrispEffects_Demo/CrispEffects_Demo.unity`
  - `CrispEffects_Demo/CrispEffects_Demo_Base.prefab`
  - `CrispEffects_Demo/CrispEffects_DemoPrefabs/`
  - `CrispEffects_Demo/CrispEffects_DemoPrefabs/CrispEffect_Demo_Variant01.prefab` through `CrispEffect_Demo_Variant50.prefab`

`Crisp Effect Particle Pack Vol. 1 Assemble Artifacts/` contains internal publishing media and release-prep files for Asset Store submission and is not part of the normal customer usage workflow.

## How To Use It

1. Open `CrispEffects_Demo/CrispEffects_Demo.unity` to preview the pack.
2. Choose a texture resolution tier based on your target quality and performance needs.
3. Choose or duplicate a starter material from `CrispEffects_Materials/` based on your render pipeline and blend mode needs.
4. Assign a crisp effect texture to your particle system, material, or effect setup.
5. If using flipbooks, enable **Texture Sheet Animation** and match the frame layout to the selected `x4`, `x8`, or `x16` flipbook texture.
